# Power BI Visualization Plan

## Visual 1: Immigrant Innovation — A Catalyst for U.S. Patents
- **Chart Type:** Line Chart with Dual Y-Axis
- **Data Fields:** Year | Immigrant Share of STEM Workers (%) | Patent Volume
- **Narrative:** Shows correlation between immigrant inventors and rising U.S. patent output since 1990

## Visual 2: Filling the Gap — Immigrants Sustain America's Workforce
- **Chart Type:** Stacked Area Chart
- **Data Fields:** Year | Native-Born Prime-Age Workers | Foreign-Born Prime-Age Workers (25-54)
- **Narrative:** Demonstrates immigrants accounted for ~75% of prime-age labor force growth (2000-2022)

## Visual 3: The Expanding Pie — Immigration's Trillion-Dollar GDP Boost
- **Chart Type:** KPI Card + Bar Chart Combo
- **Data Fields:** Year | Projected GDP Growth (Trillions) | Immigrant Tax Contributions
- **Narrative:** CBO projects $8.9 trillion GDP boost (2024-2034) from immigration-driven growth
