## GDP & Fiscal Impact
- Immigration projected to boost GDP by $8.9 trillion (2024-2034)
- Deficit reduction of $0.9 trillion over same period
- Source: CBO 2024 (via MPI)

## Patent Innovation
- Immigrants represent 16% of inventors but authored 23% of U.S. patents
- Immigrant inventors responsible for ~32% of aggregate innovation (including spillover effects)
- Source: USPTO/NBER 2022

## Labor Force Growth
- Immigrants accounted for ~75% of prime-age (25-54) labor force growth (2000-2022)
- Foreign-born share of U.S. population: ~14% (46 million people)
- Source: Census Bureau / MPI
